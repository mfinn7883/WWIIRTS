Platoon and Weapons Platoon


Platoon
	4 Squads of 12 men at full strength
		Each Squad(Full Strength=FS)
		Each Squad Takes up 1 Hex ~14 meters across)
		No 2 squads can occupy the same hex
		1 squad can never occupy 2 hexs
		
		10 Rifles
			General Attack and Defence 
			Need Ammo
		1 LMG(2 man team)
			Provide Suppresion 
				Allows for "Pinning" Enemy
				Modifies Enemies Stats while in combat
				Takes a brief amount of time to set up depeding upon unit state(Moving,Not Moving etc)
				Need Same Ammo as Rifles
	Squads Needs
		Rations
		Ammo(All the same type)



Weapons PLatoon
	4 Squads of 12 men at full strength
	2 Rifle Squads
		Each Squad(FS)
		Each Squad Takes up 1 Hex(25 meters across)
		No 2 squads can occupy the same hex
		1 squad can never occupy 2 hexs
		
		10 Rifles
			General Attack and Defence 
			Need Ammo
		1 LMG(2 man team)
			Provide Suppresion 
				Allows for "Pinning" Enemy
				Modifies Enemies Stats while in combat
				Takes a brief amount of time to set up depeding upon unit state(Moving,Not Moving etc)
				Need Same Ammo as Rifles
	Squads Needs
		Rations
		Ammo(All the same type)
		
	2 Weapons Squad
		Each Squad(FS)
		Each Squad Takes up 1 Hex(25 meters across)
		No 2 squads can occupy the same hex
		1 squad can never occupy 2 hexs
		
		4 Rifles
			General Attack and Defence 
			Need Ammo
		8 Crew(Depending On weapon Systems)
	Squads Needs
		Rations
		Ammo
		Specialized Ammo(On some Weapon systems)
	
	
Differnt Weapons Systems for Weapons Squad (Star Indicates They Require Specilized Ammo)
	Sniper Teams
		Increaased Vision While not Moving or in Recon State
		Can inflict Enemy Casualties Without Prompting Return Fire
		Lower Firepower In Normal Combat
		Movement Speed Between Mortar Squad and Rifle Squad
	Mortars*
		Can Suppress 2 Squads at Once
		Provide suppression over long range at Average Accuracy
		Slow Moving
		Long Prep Time
	Artillery*
		Provide Heavy Suppression over longer range at Poor accuracy
		Can Suppress entire Platoon
		Very Slow Moving
		Very Long Prep Time
	Demo Squad*
		Can destroy Roads/Bridges 
		Disrupt enemy supply
		Otherwise Act as Normal Rifle Squad
	Heavy Machine guns
		Provide Suppression over Medium Range
		Increase Combat Damage
		Much Higher Suppression than Rifle Squad
		Can Only Suppress 1 enemy Squad at a time
		Slower Movement
		Long Prep Time
